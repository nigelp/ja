Shri Mataji spent her life delivering Her divine message of light and love to Seekers across the planet. She constantly encouraged yogis to spread Her message whenever they could.

A Sahaja Yoga tour last visited this beautiful island in 1999, with the blessings of Shri Mataji. We believe it's time to revisit and spread the vibrations once more.

The two-week tour will visit both the North coast of the island, and also the capital Kingston. We hope that you can find it in your heart to join us vibrationally, even if not physically, on this historic return to the country.

The Tour Itinerary

The main tour will assemble in the city of Kingston on the 27th of August, however several yogis will arrive earlier to prepare the ground for the full tour, and hopefully conduct some public programs on the North coast of the island. You are welcome to join the tour at any point that suits you!

Why Jamaica?

Sahaja yoga has taken root in the Caribbean and Latin America region over the past several decades. There are now established yogis in Trinidad, Puerto Rico, Aruba and elsewhere. However, despite Jamaica being the largest English-speaking island in the area, there are no permanent established yogis living there.


We believe that with the deep historical connections between the UK, Jamaica and the slave trade, this is an oversight which Shri Mataji would like us to correct. In fact in 1999, She specifically suggested that we conduct self-realization programs across the island, which we did. However for whatever reason, Sahaja Yoga did not take root. This is our chance to rectify that situation.
